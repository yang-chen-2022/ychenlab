Interests

Yang's innovative research primarily centers around the development of advanced techniques for data-rich full-field experiments and numerical simulations in heterogeneous materials and structures. With a focus on fibre-reinforced composites, his findings hold significant implications in sectors like aerospace, nuclear energy, and transportation. His key areas of expertise include:

  • FFT solvers for micromechanics
  • X-ray computed tomography
  • Nonlinear mechanical modelling
  • Fluid flow in porous media
  • Carbon fibre and ceramic matrix composites
  • Image-processing and image-based simulation
